Introducing the Super KPI

A standard KPI only tells you what happened. A Super KPI is a living command center that lets you rewrite the future.

LEVEL 1: THE PAST

Standard KPI

The Axyo Difference: We automatically integrate historical data to reveal trends, seasonality, and anomalies. This eliminates manual data pulls and establishes a single source of truth, so teams can stop arguing about whose numbers are right and start focusing on the insights.

LEVEL 2: THE PRESENT

Contextual KPI

The Axyo Difference: We seamlessly fuse internal data (CRM, ERP) with external signals (macro indicators, weather) to explain the 'why' behind the numbers. Now, a sales dip isn't just a number; it's a story about a competitor's promotion and rising fuel prices.

LEVEL 3: THE FUTURE

Predictive KPI

The Axyo Difference: We generate transparent, driver-based forecasts, not black-box predictions. This builds trust and gives teams early warnings on risks and opportunities, turning them from reactive firefighters into proactive pilots.

LEVEL 4: THE ACTION

Super KPI

The Axyo Difference: We turn the KPI into an interactive control knob. Teams can simulate decisions before they're made, instantly seeing the dollar impact of pulling different levers and turning strategy into an evidence-based playbook.
